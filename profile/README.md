## Currenari

Currenari, also referred to as Currenari Lab, is an independent electronics lab and publishing imprint.

The work centres on designing, building, and documenting embedded electronic systems as complete reference builds. Projects are developed end to end, from early prototyping through to finished hardware and firmware.

More information and published work:
- https://currenari.com
- https://www.amazon.com/author/currenari

### Projects

- **Spectrum One**
